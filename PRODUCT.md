# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Early-stage biotech research teams: principal investigators, research directors, CSOs, and bench scientists at companies with 5–50 people. They work fast, read constantly, and are perpetually behind on their field. They are sophisticated, skeptical of hype, and trust precision over claims. They use Sygnifi during focused work sessions — Monday mornings, pre-meeting prep, investor briefing prep.

## Product Purpose

Sygnifi monitors the scientific field on behalf of a research team: new papers, patent filings, competitor signals, clinical trial updates. It synthesizes these through the lens of the team's specific research and delivers a weekly brief, grounded in their actual work rather than broad keyword lists. The goal is to keep researchers aware without adding to their reading burden.

## Brand Personality

Clear, rigorous, human. Scientific credibility without academic stuffiness. Feels like the smartest colleague who already read everything this week and can tell you what matters. Never hypes, never hedges.

## Anti-references

- Pharma enterprise: stock-photo scientists, sanitized corporate blues, clip-art pipeline icons, generic "partnership" imagery
- Academic paper styling: walls of text, no visual rhythm, impenetrable density, prestige without usability
- Generic AI SaaS: purple gradients, animated metric dashboards, "AI-powered" as a badge rather than a capability

## Design Principles

1. **Show the work.** The product report is the strongest proof point. Bring it forward, don't bury it below three sections of explanation.
2. **Credibility through specificity.** Vague claims ("stay informed") earn less trust than concrete, named examples (KRAS G12C, patent filed Tuesday). Use real scientific language.
3. **Human without warmth theater.** The voice is warm because it is direct and honest, not because it uses friendly illustrations or rounded corners everywhere.
4. **Restraint signals expertise.** The best practitioners trust the spare, not the decorated. Visual complexity is not a proxy for capability.
5. **Science-adjacent, not science-cosplay.** The design can draw from editorial and journal references without becoming a PDF.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Color contrast must pass for body and UI text. Reduced motion should be respected (prefers-reduced-motion). No dependence on color alone for meaning.
